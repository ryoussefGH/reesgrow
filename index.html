2. Strategic Context: Market Segmentation Index

Our strategy categorizes targets into 5 distinct regulatory tiers. This framework dictates the specific compliance solution (Validation vs. Monitoring) and pricing model required for each prospect.

Segment A

Pharma & Biotech Manufacturing

Focus: CGT, Biologics production. Drivers: FDA, cGMP.

Segment B

Pharma Labs, CROs, Reference Labs

Focus: R&D Testing. Drivers: GLP, Audits.

Segment C

Very Highly Regulated Healthcare

Focus: LN2, Specialty Pharma, Cleanrooms. Drivers: USP <797>/<800>.

Segment D

Highly Regulated Healthcare

Focus: Tissue & Blood Banks. Drivers: AABB, AATB.

Segment E

Low Regulated

Focus: Pathology Labs, Vaccine Storage, Patient Monitoring.

3. Strategic Pillars: Capitalizing on Trends

The growth strategy is driven by three primary mandates aligned with the highest compliance segments (A, B, C, D).

1. CGT & Pharma Manufacturing

Trend: Rapid cleanroom construction driven by Cell & Gene Therapy (CGT) investment.

Primary Target: Segment A

2. Modernization & Cloud

Trend: Replacement of legacy/paper systems to meet new data integrity rules (Annex 11 / 21 CFR Part 11).

Primary Target: Segment B

3. Healthcare Compliance

Trend: Mandatory monitoring for compounding pharmacies (C) and tissue storage (D).

Primary Target: Segment C & D
